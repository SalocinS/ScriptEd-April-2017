Teleportation

Description

With this mean of transportation, teleportation will allow passengers to re-locate to another location in a matter of seconds. This will be one of the quickest ways of getting from one place to another. In order for this to successfully work, there will need to be many teleportation stations, similar to the MTA train stations, where riders can casually go from one spot to the other.
