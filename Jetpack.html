Jetpack

Description

As previously mentioned, a jetpack is a contraption that sits over the riders shoulders and propels them into the air by means of water propulsion and jet propulsion. The jetpack will be able to be carried around wherever you go which allows for quick means of transport whenever the rider desires. The jetpack can last for several hours, allowing longer travel times.
